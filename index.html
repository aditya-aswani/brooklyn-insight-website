About Brooklyn Sangha

Welcome to Brooklyn Insight Sangha, a vibrant Buddhist meditation community nestled in the heart of Brooklyn. Since our founding in 2022, we've created a sanctuary where ancient wisdom meets modern life, offering a space for practitioners of all levels to explore meditation and Buddhist teachings.

Our community draws from various Buddhist traditions, primarily Theravada and Zen, creating an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives enrich our collective practice. Whether you're taking your first steps into meditation or deepening an established practice, you'll find support and guidance here.

Our Mission

To provide accessible Buddhist meditation instruction and create a supportive community for spiritual growth in Brooklyn. We believe in the transformative power of mindfulness and compassion to bring peace to individuals and our broader community.

What We Offer

  • Weekly guided meditation sessions
  • Dharma talks and discussions
  • Day-long retreats and workshops
  • One-on-one meditation instruction
  • Community service opportunities
